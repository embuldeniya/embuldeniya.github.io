Workflow

From consultation to
complete record in minutes

Record
Doctor starts recording the consultation — audio is captured securely in the browser.
Transcribe
AI speech recognition converts audio to text — supporting Sinhala, Tamil, and English.
Summarise
Gemini AI generates a structured clinical note — history, examination, diagnoses, and plan.
Review & save
Doctor reviews, edits, and approves the summary — then saves, prints, or refers.

NESTOR.AI: A Native AI Physician Assistant for Multilingual Clinical Documentation in Sri Lanka
H.U. Illangakoon, T. Embuldeniya
NESTOR.AI is a locally developed AI-Physician Assistant designed for Sri Lankan outpatient settings. The platform utilises multilingual speech recognition and AI-assisted clinical summarisation to convert doctor–patient conversations into structured medical documentation, supporting Sinhala, Tamil, and English consultations. The system generates clinical notes, prescriptions, referral letters, and searchable digital records, while providing AI clinical decision support and drug safety alerts.
