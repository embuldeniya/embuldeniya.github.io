AI Engine

A locally optimised
clinical language model

Nestor.AI is powered by a locally fine-tuned large language model, purpose-built for Sri Lankan clinical documentation. Rather than relying on generic AI responses, every output is shaped by locally aligned clinical prompts that encode the structure, standards, and language patterns of Sri Lankan outpatient medicine.

The model understands the nuances of multilingual consultations, translating Sinhala and Tamil clinical narratives into precise, structured English documentation, while remaining grounded in locally relevant drug lists, investigation references, and diagnostic terminology.

1
Clinical structure modeling
The model enforces a SOAP-style note structure — chief complaint, history, examination, investigations, assessment, and plan — automatically, every time.
2
Sri Lankan medical vocabulary
A custom speech context featuring 300+ locally relevant drug names, investigations, and clinical terms across Sinhala, Tamil, and English.
3
Safety-first model constraints
Hard-coded guardrails that force the model to flag drug interactions, allergy contraindications, and missing diagnoses before the output is finalised.
4
Iterative refinement loop
Doctors can append additional notes, scanned documents, or corrections, and the model re-summarises the full context with each update.

Academic Conference Poster · Sri Lanka · 2026
NESTOR.AI: A Native AI Physician Assistant for Multilingual Clinical Documentation in Sri Lanka
Dr. Helix , T. Embuldeniya
NESTOR.AI is a locally developed AI Physician Assistant designed for Sri Lankan outpatient settings. The platform utilises multilingual speech recognition and locally deployed AI, incorporating locally aligned clinical prompts, to convert doctor–patient conversations into structured medical documentation. It supports Sinhala, Tamil, and English consultations, generating clinical notes, prescriptions, referral letters, and searchable digital records, while providing AI-driven clinical decision support and drug safety alerts.
